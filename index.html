About — what I do

I work at the intersection of remote sensing, geospatial science and machine learning to deliver actionable agricultural intelligence. I build automated geospatial pipelines, perform satellite image analysis (optical & SAR), and integrate results with crop models to support decision-making and policy.

Current focus: crop mapping and yield estimation, irrigation and watershed assessment, and operational geospatial monitoring on cloud platforms (Google Earth Engine).
